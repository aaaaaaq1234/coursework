On this page, there will be information about the events that are going to be taking place during the festival, there is going to be 5 different events happening in this festival, there will be 5 events happening for you to go to if you like any of our chosen events for you, this festival should be fun for everyone visiting it

The first event that will be happening in this festival is claymaking which is where you can make and decorate your own objects with clay, once you have molded your clay into the shape that you want it to be, you will have to wait for your clay to be dried so the clay stays in the shape and then you will be able to do whatever you want to with your model

The second event that will be happening in this festival is facepainting, this is where a professional face painters will show you a list of designs for you to choose for you to choose from and once you pick the one you want to have done you have to sit still and don't move until the face painter is finished with your design and then you can walk around with your new face paint

The third event that will be happening in this festival is dress making, this is where you make fabric dresses by sewing the dress together with your sewing skills but don't worry if your not good at sewing we will have staff there to help you when your struggling to sew the dress together and once you have finished sewing the dress you can buy the dress you made and take it home with you as a memory of the festival
